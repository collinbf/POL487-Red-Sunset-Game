About this project

A turn-based strategy simulation built for POL487: Soviet Collapse. You play as Mikhail Gorbachev, taking office in March 1985 with a crumbling economy, a restless empire, and a party resistant to change.

Each turn is one month. Policies are chosen. Events unfold. Advisors shift their loyalties. The question is not whether you can save the USSR — it is whether you can understand why it fell.

All content — events, policies, advisors, and encyclopedia entries — was researched and written by students.
